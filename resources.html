Resources

TED Talks

A global set of conferences run by the private non-profit organization Sapling Foundation, under the slogan "Ideas Worth Spreading".

  • Inspiring speeches
  • Wide range of topics
  • Free access to talks
  • Global community

WikiHow

An online wiki-style community-edited encyclopedia of how-to guides.

  • Step-by-step guides
  • Practical advice
  • Diverse topics
  • Community contributions

Wikipedia

A free, open-source online encyclopedia created and maintained by a community of volunteers worldwide.

  • Millions of articles
  • Multilingual content
  • Community-edited
  • Free knowledge resource

Wiktionary

A multilingual, web-based project to create a free content dictionary of terms in all natural languages and a few constructed languages.

  • Multilingual dictionary
  • Etymologies & pronunciations
  • Thesaurus & translations
  • Free and open content

Wikitravel

A free, open-source travel guide written by volunteers, covering destinations worldwide.

  • Travel guides
  • Destination information
  • Phrasebooks & itineraries
  • Community-driven content

Fandom

A wiki hosting service that hosts wikis on a wide variety of topics, primarily entertainment-related.

  • Fan-made wikis
  • Deep dives into pop culture
  • Movies, games, TV shows
  • Community discussions

The Spruce Crafts

Provides expert advice and inspiration for all types of crafts, from knitting to paper crafts and more.

  • Crafting tutorials
  • DIY project ideas
  • Material guides
  • Creative inspiration

How-To Geek

Offers easy-to-understand articles and guides on technology, software, and various how-to topics.

  • Tech tutorials
  • Software guides
  • Troubleshooting tips
  • Consumer tech reviews
