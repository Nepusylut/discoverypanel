音乐平台

Discover a diverse range of music platforms from around the world, including global streaming giants and popular Chinese services. Whether you're looking for vast music libraries, personalized recommendations, or unique social music experiences, this overview covers platforms for every music lover.

Foreign Music Platforms

Spotify

A leading global music streaming service offering a vast library of songs, podcasts, and personalized playlists.

  • Extensive music library
  • Personalized playlists
  • Podcast integration
  • Offline listening

Apple Music

Apple's music streaming service with a massive catalog, exclusive content, and seamless integration with Apple devices.

  • Huge music catalog
  • Exclusive content
  • Spatial Audio
  • Lyrics view

YouTube Music

Google's music streaming service offering official songs, albums, playlists, and live performances, alongside YouTube's vast video catalog.

  • Official music videos
  • Live performances
  • Personalized mixes
  • Ad-free with Premium

Amazon Music

Amazon's music streaming service, offering millions of songs, HD and Ultra HD audio, and integrated with Alexa devices.

  • HD & Ultra HD audio
  • Alexa integration
  • Ad-free streaming
  • Curated playlists

Tidal

A music streaming service known for its high-fidelity sound, high-quality music videos, and artist-centric approach with exclusive content.

  • High-fidelity audio
  • High-quality music videos
  • Exclusive content
  • Artist payouts

Deezer

A global music streaming service offering a large catalog, personalized recommendations, and Flow, an infinite mix of your favorites.

  • Large music catalog
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Flow feature
  • Lyrics on demand

Pandora

A personalized internet radio service that uses the Music Genome Project to create stations based on your favorite songs or artists.

  • Personalized radio stations
  • Music Genome Project
  • Podcast streaming
  • Limited skips (free tier)

SoundCloud

An open audio platform for creators to upload, share, and promote their music and audio, featuring a vast collection of independent artists.

  • Independent artist focus
  • Audio sharing platform
  • New music discovery
  • Creator tools

Napster

A pioneering digital music service that has evolved into a subscription-based streaming platform with a large music catalog.

  • Large music catalog
  • Subscription streaming
  • Offline playback
  • Curated content

Bandcamp

An online music store and community for independent artists to sell and stream their music directly to fans, supporting artists financially.

  • Artist-first platform
  • Direct artist support
  • High-quality audio
  • Digital & physical sales

China Music Platforms

QQ音乐

One of China's largest music streaming services by Tencent Music, offering a vast library, social features, and live music content.

  • Massive music library
  • Social sharing
  • Live music streaming
  • Karaoke features

网易云音乐

Known for its strong social features, user-generated playlists, and vibrant music community, popular among younger audiences in China.

  • Strong social community
  • User-generated playlists
  • Music reviews & comments
  • Independent music focus

酷狗音乐

A popular Chinese music streaming and download service, known for its extensive music library and integrated KTV features.

  • Large music library
  • Music downloads
  • Integrated KTV
  • Live streaming

酷我音乐

Another major music streaming platform in China, offering high-quality audio, curated playlists, and a focus on popular music.

  • High-quality audio
  • Curated playlists
  • Popular music focus
  • Offline playback

咪咕音乐

Operated by China Mobile, Migu Music offers a comprehensive music streaming service with a focus on licensed content and telecommunication integration.

  • Licensed music content
  • Telecom integration
  • Concert streaming
  • Offline downloads

全民K歌

A popular mobile karaoke app from Tencent, allowing users to sing, record, and share their performances, with social interaction features.

  • Mobile karaoke app
  • Record & share songs
  • Social interaction
  • Vast song library

千千音乐

Formerly Baidu Music, Qianqian Music is a streaming service offering a wide range of music, focusing on a clean interface and user experience.

  • Wide music selection
  • Clean interface
  • User-friendly experience
  • Offline playback

华为音乐

Huawei's official music streaming service, offering a diverse music library, high-quality audio, and seamless integration with Huawei devices.

  • Diverse music library
  • High-quality audio
  • Huawei device integration
  • Personalized recommendations
