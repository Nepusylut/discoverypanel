Explore a curated list of prominent online learning platforms, categorized by creative and professional skills. Each card provides a direct link to the respective platform, along with its logo icon, a brief description of its purpose, and key features.

Creative Skills Platforms

The Futur

An online education platform providing business and design education for creatives.

  • Design and business courses
  • Free YouTube content
  • Community and mentorship
  • Tools and resources for creatives

Behance

Adobe's online platform to showcase and discover creative work, connecting artists with opportunities.

  • Portfolio hosting
  • Creative job board
  • Live streams from creators
  • Discover work by category/field

Envato Tuts+

An online learning platform offering thousands of tutorials, courses, and eBooks on creative and technical skills.

  • Extensive library of tutorials
  • Courses on various creative software
  • E-books and guides
  • Focus on practical, hands-on learning

Skillshare

An online learning community with thousands of classes for creative and curious people, on topics including illustration, design, photography, video, freelancing, and more.

  • Project-based classes
  • Community feedback on projects
  • Subscription-based access
  • Wide range of creative topics

Professional Skills Platforms

Harvard Business Review (HBR)

A general-management magazine published by Harvard Business Publishing, offering insights and best practices for business leaders.

  • In-depth articles and case studies
  • Leadership and management insights
  • Research and analysis
  • Podcasts and webinars

Getting Things Done (GTD)

A popular time management method created by David Allen, focused on organizing tasks and improving productivity.

  • Methodology for productivity
  • Workshops and coaching
  • Books and resources
  • Focus on actionable steps

Lifehack

A productivity and lifestyle blog offering tips, tricks, and advice on improving various aspects of life, from work to personal habits.

  • Productivity tips and hacks
  • Personal development advice
  • Time management strategies
  • Articles on various life skills

MIT OpenCourseWare

A free and open online publication of educational materials from MIT courses, available to self-learners worldwide.

  • Free access to MIT course materials
  • Lectures, notes, and assignments
  • Wide range of academic subjects
  • No credits or degrees awarded

Google Digital Garage

A program by Google offering free online courses and certifications in digital marketing, career development, and data.

  • Free online courses
  • Accredited certifications
  • Digital marketing skills
  • Career development modules

Coursera

An online learning platform offering courses, specializations, and degrees from universities and companies worldwide.

  • Courses from top universities
  • Professional certificates
  • MasterTrack and online degrees
  • Flexible learning schedules
