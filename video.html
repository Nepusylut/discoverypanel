视频平台

Discover a diverse range of video platforms from around the world, including global streaming giants and popular Chinese services. Whether you're looking for movies, TV shows, documentaries, or user-generated content, this overview covers platforms for every video enthusiast.

Foreign Video Platforms

Netflix

A global streaming service offering a vast library of movies, TV shows, and documentaries, including award-winning originals.

  • Original content
  • Diverse genres
  • Offline downloads
  • Multiple profiles

Disney+

The streaming home for movies and shows from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic.

  • Disney classics
  • Marvel Cinematic Universe
  • Star Wars saga
  • Pixar animations

HBO Max

A premium streaming service featuring content from HBO, Warner Bros., DC, Cartoon Network, and more.

  • HBO originals
  • Warner Bros. movies
  • DC Universe
  • Max exclusives

Hulu

Offers a wide selection of current and classic TV shows, movies, and Hulu Originals, with live TV options available.

  • Current TV shows
  • Hulu Originals
  • Live TV option
  • Ad-supported & ad-free plans

Tubi

A free, ad-supported streaming service offering a vast library of movies and TV shows from various studios.

  • Free to watch
  • Large movie & TV library
  • No subscription needed
  • Ad-supported

Peacock

NBCUniversal's streaming service, featuring movies, TV shows, news, and sports, with a free tier available.

  • NBC shows
  • Universal movies
  • Live sports
  • Free tier available

Paramount+

Streams live sports, breaking news, and a mountain of entertainment from CBS, BET, Comedy Central, MTV, Nickelodeon, and Smithsonian Channel.

  • Live sports
  • Breaking news
  • CBS shows
  • Originals & exclusives

Plex

A global streaming media service and client–server media player platform, offering a vast library of free movies, TV shows, and live TV channels.

  • Free movies & TV
  • Live TV channels
  • Personal media server
  • Ad-supported

Vudu

Offers movies and TV shows for rent or purchase, with a selection of free, ad-supported content.

  • Rent/buy movies
  • Free movies with ads
  • New releases
  • 4K UHD support

IMDb TV (Freevee)

Amazon's free streaming service with thousands of movies and TV shows, including Amazon Originals.

  • Free with ads
  • Amazon Originals
  • Popular movies & TV
  • No subscription

Amazon Prime Video

Amazon's video streaming service included with Prime membership, offering a wide range of movies, TV shows, and exclusive originals.

  • Included with Prime
  • Amazon Originals
  • Rent/buy options
  • Offline downloads

YouTube Premium

Offers ad-free access to all YouTube videos, background playback, downloads, and exclusive content.

  • Ad-free YouTube
  • Background playback
  • Offline downloads
  • YouTube Originals

Crunchyroll

The ultimate destination for anime and manga, offering a vast library of simulcast series and classic titles.

  • Largest anime library
  • Simulcasts
  • Manga access
  • Multiple languages

Miruro

Miruro TV offers a wide variety of Asian dramas, movies, and TV shows with subtitles, primarily focusing on Chinese content.

  • Asian dramas & movies
  • Chinese content focus
  • Subtitled content
  • Regularly updated

China Video Platforms

腾讯视频 (Tencent Video)

One of China's largest online video platforms, offering a wide range of movies, TV series, variety shows, and animations.

  • Massive content library
  • Exclusive dramas
  • Live streaming
  • VIP membership

爱奇艺 (iQIYI)

A leading online video platform in China, known for its high-quality original productions and diverse content categories.

  • Original productions
  • Diverse content
  • Interactive features
  • Dolby Vision support

优酷 (Youku)

A major Chinese online video platform, offering a rich collection of movies, TV series, and user-generated content.

  • Rich content library
  • User-generated content
  • Live events
  • Multi-device support

芒果tv (Mango TV)

The official streaming platform of Hunan Broadcasting System, specializing in popular variety shows and exclusive dramas.

  • Exclusive variety shows
  • Hunan TV dramas
  • Live broadcasts
  • Interactive voting

哔哩哔哩 (Bilibili)

A popular video sharing platform in China, especially among younger generations, known for anime, comics, games (ACG) content, and user-generated videos.

  • ACG content
  • User-generated videos
  • Bullet comments (弹幕)
  • Live streaming

搜狐视频 (Sohu Video)

Sohu's online video platform, offering a variety of movies, TV series, news, and entertainment content.

  • Movies & TV series
  • News & entertainment
  • Original web series
  • Free content

AcFun

One of China's earliest ACG (Animation, Comics, Games) content sharing platforms, similar to Bilibili but with its own unique community.

  • ACG community
  • User-uploaded videos
  • Live streaming
  • Danmu comments

1905电影网

The official website of China Movie Channel (CCTV-6), offering a vast library of Chinese and international movies, news, and film-related content.

  • Movie library
  • Film news
  • Exclusive interviews
  • Online screenings

爱外剧 (Aiwaiju)

A platform primarily focused on foreign TV series, offering a wide range of international dramas with Chinese subtitles.

  • Foreign TV series
  • Chinese subtitles
  • Regular updates
  • Diverse genres

爱一帆 (Aiyifan)

An online platform for watching foreign movies and TV shows, often providing quick updates on popular international content.

  • Foreign movies & TV
  • Fast updates
  • User-friendly interface
  • Various resolutions

华视界 (Huashijie)

A platform offering a selection of Chinese and international films and TV series, with a focus on high-quality streaming.

  • Chinese & international content
  • High-quality streaming
  • Diverse categories
  • Regular additions

优视频 (You Video)

An online video platform providing a variety of movies, TV series, and short videos, often featuring user-contributed content.

  • Short videos
  • User-contributed content
